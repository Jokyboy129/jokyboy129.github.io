Welcome

Welcome to my personal website.
Here I publish all of my apps in one place. All of them are offered for free download.
All apps provided work perfectly with screenreaders like NVDA or JAWS. They are available for Windows users only and only in German and English.

Please note: Since this is a hobby project, development may progress a bit slower.
